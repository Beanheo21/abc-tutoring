About Us

ABC Tutoring was founded with the mission to support students in underserved areas by providing high-quality tutoring services at a low cost or for no cost at all. ABC tutoring focuses on fostering and nurturing the next generation and aims to promote the following principles through our tutoring service:

  • Curiosity: Encouraging a love for learning and exploration.
  • Determination: Fostering perseverance in overcoming academic challenges.
  • Patience: Understanding that growth is a process and supporting students every step of the way.
  • Empathy: Connecting with students to support their emotional and academic needs.
  • Responsibility: Modeling integrity, reliability, and accountability.
  • Creativity: Using imaginative methods to make learning engaging and fun.
  • Clarity: Ensuring explanations are clear and easy to follow.

FAQ

How do I book a session? 1. Click the "Book a Session" tab and fill in the blanks. 2. Once completed, submit the form. 3. A team member will reach out within 3 days to schedule a meeting time for the child.

What ages do you tutor? We specialize in tutoring elementary school students.

What subjects do you offer? Math and English.

How long is each session? Typically 30 to 45 minutes depending on needs.

Are your tutors qualified? All tutors are high-achieving high school students trained to work with younger learners.

How much does it cost? Tutoring is free or low-cost to increase accessibility.

What platform do you use for tutoring sessions? We typically use Zoom or Google Meet. You'll receive a link once a session is confirmed.

Can parents sit in during the session? Yes! We welcome parent involvement, especially for younger students.

How often can my child receive tutoring? Students can typically book one to two sessions per week, depending on tutor availability.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule a session? Just email us at least 24 hours in advance, and we’ll do our best to reschedule.

Will the same tutor work with my child each time? We try to keep tutoring consistent, but availability may vary week to week.

Can you help with homework? Yes! We can help with current assignments and also offer practice and review on key topics.

Can I request a specific tutor? You can note preferences in the booking form, and we’ll try to match accordingly.

Is there a waitlist? Occasionally, yes — but we’ll notify you within a few days if there’s a delay in scheduling.
